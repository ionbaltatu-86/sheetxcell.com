How this loan payment calculator works

This calculator estimates your monthly loan payment using the loan amount, annual interest rate, and loan term. It also shows the total amount paid over time and the total interest cost.

Example

If you borrow $20,000 at 7% interest for 5 years, your monthly payment will stay fixed if the rate and term remain unchanged. You can use this tool to compare different terms and see how much interest you may pay.
