How this debt payoff calculator works

This calculator estimates how long it may take to pay off a debt based on your current balance, interest rate, and monthly payment. It also estimates total interest paid over the payoff period.

Example

If you owe $40,000 at 7.44% interest and pay $500 per month, this tool can estimate how many months it may take to become debt-free and how much total interest you could pay.
